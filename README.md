# SehatSetu

सेहत + सेतु | Health Bridge

AI-powered rural health intelligence platform for ASHA workers across India's last mile.

"Know who to visit. Know what to look for. Before it's too late."

---

## 🚨 Problem

In rural India, patients exist. Clinics exist. Medicines exist.
But the intelligence connecting them does not.

* 40% of discharged patients develop complications without follow-up
* 4 in 10 rural women suffer from undetected anemia
* 63% of chronic patients stop medication silently

---

## 💡 Solution

SehatSetu turns a basic smartphone into a **predictive health assistant** for ASHA workers.

### 🔹 RiskSense

* Predicts high-risk patients post-discharge
* Generates daily prioritized visit list
* Outputs risk score + reason

### 🔹 VisionCare

* Detects anemia using smartphone camera
* Instant screening in seconds
* Works offline, no extra hardware
